Navigating in 3D

You can set the camera position directly by tpying in numbers. Alternatively, you can navigate with keyboard and mouse. For this, the render window has to be in the foreground.

Input Navigation
W, A, S, D keys Move forward, left, backwards or right. If you press the shift key, you move faster.
Click and drag The camera rotates around the current target position.
Click and drag with the secondary mouse button or pressed control key The camera rotates around its own position.
Click and drag with pressed shift key The camera moves up, down, left or right.
