Finding models to use

GLLara can open all models that were designed for the Windows apps XNALara or XPS. It can also open most files in OBJ format.

The original XNALara models can be found at http://www.tombraiderforums.com/showthread.php?t=147100. Additional models can, for example, be found at http://www.tombraiderforums.com/showthread.php?t=151335.
